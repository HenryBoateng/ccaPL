Now that we have gone over the basics of CCA, here are a few questions to try out.
The following process sends a hello message and receives an acknowledgement:

send(hello).recv(x).0 | P

Give the specification of P.
write a program that makes ambient 'Henry' always looking to recieve information regarding 'sender, recipient and message' from another sibling ambients. Note: the output should be empty
Re-create this: m and n are sibling ambients, u is child ambient to n, w is child ambient to m, u sends a message hi to w.
Modify the previous program to allow w to send an acknowledgement thanks to u upon receiving the message.
